The Founder

VK · Lead PhotographerEst. 2018

Trained the lens on people since 2018.

VK started as a second shooter at Hyderabad weddings, carrying one body for wide coverage and a second for the close, unrepeatable moments — the ring exchange, the glance between parents, the exhale after the vows. That habit became the studio's whole method.

Today VK leads every shoot personally, working two mirrorless bodies at once so a wedding, portrait sitting, or brand campaign is covered from both a documentary and an editorial distance — without asking anyone to pose twice.
